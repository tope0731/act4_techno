About Our Startup

  • 2023

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We saw kids focusing more on phones than studying, inspiring our platform idea. With many children using phones, we saw a learning opportunity. Since kids enjoy learning on phones, we're creating a fun learning platform for them. Our goal is to help kids read and write using phones, addressing parental struggles with digital distractions. We'll offer phone-friendly reading and writing materials to aid parents in supporting their kids' learning. Through interactive learning, we aim to enhance both digital and traditional literacy skills.

  • March 2024

    Our mission

    To develop a reading and writing application for young children to become confident readers and writers through a fun, personalized learning system that sparks a lifelong love of literacy and guides parents on their child's educational journey.

  • March 2024

    Vision

    To empower young children to become confident readers and writers through a fun, personalized learning system that engages them and guides parents on their learning journey by 2026

Letter Writing Recognition


Our letter writing recognition feature offers a convenient solution for transforming handwritten letters into digital format with remarkable accuracy. Users can effortlessly digitize their handwritten correspondence simply by writing or tracing letters on any compatible device's touchscreen.

Numbers Writing Recognition


Our number writing recognition feature offers a seamless solution for converting handwritten numbers into digital format with remarkable accuracy. Users can effortlessly digitize numerical data by writing or tracing numbers on any compatible touchscreen device.
